Why You Need a Website

  • Your Customers Expect It: Over 90% of customers search online before purchasing.
  • 24/7 Global Reach: Your website is always available to help customers anytime, anywhere.
  • Build Trust: A good website helps people believe in your business.
  • Stay in Control: You decide what people see and read about your brand.

How a Website Grows Your Business

  • Attracts new customers online through Google.
  • Show your products, services, prices, and location etc.
  • Provide 24/7 support with forms, chatbots, and FAQs.
  • Compete with other brands.
  • Build trust with customer.

What You Are Missing Without a Website

  • Hard to find your shop/café/clinic etc online.
  • Losing customers to competitors who already have website.
  • No online feedback or Google reviews.
  • No professional look on the internet.
  • You might lose customers if you depend only on walk-ins or referrals.
  • People may not trust your business if you don't have a website.
